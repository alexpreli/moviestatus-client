Transparency Report

MovieStatus is committed to being transparent about its operations. This report describes how we handle legal requests and metadata.


Metadata Sources

All movie and TV series metadata is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDb). Streaming availability results are sourced from official APIs provided by the respective platforms.


Legal Requests

We receive very few legal requests. We take each request seriously and review it before taking any action. We prioritize the privacy and security of our users.


Financial Disclosures

MovieStatus is an independent project. We do not receive funding from any government or political organization. We do not have any external investors.
